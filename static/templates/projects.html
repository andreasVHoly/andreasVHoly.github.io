BantuWeb

My honours project involves making the Web portal and corresponding back-end for a digital archive for resource scare South African languages. More about the project can be found on our website. My role in the project involves creating a Web portal for users to explore the content and for them to upload their own content in resource scare languages. Gamification plays a big role, as I am researching how Gamification can influence users to contribute more content. On the front-end I am using AngularJS and Bootstrap which is working together with a python Django back-end. I purposefully chose this part of the project as in our undergrad and honours years no Web development was covered and saw this as an opportunity to get into this important field. Even though it has been a learning curve of note, I have enjoyed it a lot and pride myself in how much I have achieved in this field in such a short time for the project.
